## 3. Competitor inspiration — Nervous System Reboot Summit

**Sources:** `03-competitor-value-props.png`, `04-competitor-landing-page.png`
**Ask:** *"Extra info from a nervous-system course I found online. Maybe we can pull something from it."*

Site: `www.nervoussystemrebootsummit.com`
Event: "NERVOUS SYSTEM REBOOT — The Me I Used To Be" (May 26–31, 2026)

### Patterns observed

**Headline / hook:**
- "Move out of survival mode and feel more like yourself again"
- Empathetic subhead: "You've done the work — but your body still holds tension. Experience practices that calm your nervous system, reduce reactivity, and restore clarity and steadiness."

**Three outcome bullets (checkmark-style):**
- Shift out of fight, flight, or freeze when stress hits
- Release tension, so clarity and energy return
- Respond with calm, even in tense moments

**Social proof:**
- "Join Bessel van der Kolk, Peter Levine, Arielle Schwartz, and 40+ world-renowned experts in neuroscience, trauma healing, somatics, and integrative health."

**Feature list (emoji + short):**
- Gentle daily embodiment practices
- Science-backed nervous system guidance
- Tools to shift stress in real time

**CTA:** "Reserve My Free Spot" (single strong verb, action-forward)

### Tone caveat

This is a **free, mass-market online summit** pitching to a broad nervous-system-curious audience. The Care Ranch is a **premium, intimate, in-person retreat** for senior leaders. Mechanically copying their breezy-CTA / hero-bullet style would cheapen our positioning. Selectively adopting the *outcome-focused clarity* and the *empathetic acknowledgment of the reader's starting state* is fair game.
